Site Plan: Global Soccer Stars

Site Name

Name: Global Soccer Stars

Reason: This name reflects the website's focus on showcasing the most prominent soccer players worldwide. It is simple, recognizable, and relevant to the topic of soccer players.

Optional Domain: globalsoccerstars.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an engaging platform for soccer enthusiasts to explore and learn about their favorite soccer players. The site will feature player profiles, career statistics, awards, achievements, and notable moments. It will also include an interactive section for fan engagement, such as polls or quizzes.

Color Schema

Primary Color: Green (#008000) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and accents.

Secondary Color: White (#FFFFFF) - Used for body and main content background.

Accent Color: Gold (#FFD700) - Highlights awards and call-to-action buttons.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Roboto Slab - Used for headings such as player names and section titles.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - Used for player descriptions and general content.
  • Accent Font: Montserrat - Used for navigation links and buttons.
